About Us

What is Technology Business Incubator?

Technology Business Incubator (TBI) VSSUT Burla is a non-profit start-up incubator which aspires to promote entrepreneurship in the state by nurturing and guiding the budding startups. Recognised as one of the eminent TBIs in the region with great potential, it has been working tirelessly and persistently to turn innovative ideas into successful startups. Furthermore, it has collaborated with Dept of Science & Technology, Dept of Biotechnology, National Innovation Foundation, and regarded foreign universities to stimulate an eclectic ecosystem conducive to innovation and accelerate entrepreneurial ventures. With the aim of producing a sustainable innovation ecosystem and entrepreneurial environment, TBI VSSUT focuses on boosting blooming startups and makes them ready for the competitive and ever-changing environment.

TBI in VSSUT,Burla

Since two years, TBI VSSUT is active in uplifting innovation and entrepreneurship in the region. Through the contacts with distinguished universities and industries,we have incubated many startups Within this short period, our TBI has worked in the arena of Aerospace, Education and skill development, Agriculture, Healthcare and biotechnology, 3D printing and Robotics, and other Grassroot Innovations;

Objectives

TBI VSSUT seeks to convert Odisha into a global hub for entrepreneurial ventures. We aim to promote research, innovation and entrepreneurship in the state and bridge the gap between an idea and its implementation.. We aim to inculcate the entrepreneurial mindset and problem solving attitude in the budding technocrats and initiate startup enterprises. Providing a sustainable and supportable environment for the techno-enthusiasts remains our motto.
